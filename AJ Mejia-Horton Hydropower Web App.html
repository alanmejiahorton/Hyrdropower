Spring Operations

Spring-Level Stabilization

The spring stabilization rule protects recreation, shoreline habitat, fish spawning windows, and downstream minimum-flow reliability by limiting discretionary drawdown at Lake James, Lake Norman, Lake Wylie, and Lake Wateree.

  • Reduces modeled water availability to hold storage in target bands.
  • Lowers dispatch flexibility during high-value hours when storage needs protection.
  • Raises hydropower value when usable storage becomes operationally scarce.
