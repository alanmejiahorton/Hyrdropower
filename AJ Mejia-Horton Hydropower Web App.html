Sources And Assumptions

The model uses the user-provided outline for feature scope, the user-provided data PDF for reservoir storage scale estimates, and FERC's November 25, 2015 order for licensed capacity, project configuration, operating guide-curve notes, recreation facilities, and environmental measures.

  • FERC order: new 40-year license issued November 25, 2015; total authorized installed capacity is 819.102 MW.
  • Research mode is based on the provided robust MPC paper: 4-week rolling horizon, 24-hour commitments, inflow/LMP box uncertainty, emergency shortage/spillage recourse, and Bridgewater-focused validation.
  • Duke page: non-federal hydro projects relicense under the Federal Power Act, generally for 30-50 years.
  • Simulation math is a planning proxy, not a SCADA, CHEOPS, Pyomo, or FERC compliance engine.
